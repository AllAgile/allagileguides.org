We are committed to providing high-quality Creative Commons audio recordings of Agile Guides, including the Scrum Guide. We believe that knowledge comes from experiences and audio versions of Agile Guides helps to grow knowledge.

The podcast is read by John Albrecht from All Agile Limited and Scrum Lake

Scrum Lake is an interative Co-Creative workshop for Scrum Masters from all over the world to connect with each other. In our workshops we share stories, discuss techniques and take a deeper dive into Scrum theory, practice and reality.
